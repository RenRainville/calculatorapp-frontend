IAAC MaCAD 2022/23 Thesis

by Ren Rainville & James McBennett

Thesis Supervisor: David Andrés León

Our thesis project uses cloud-based data-management and graph-machine learning to quickly predict a building from a open or closed spline inputted by a user in a web-browser. A downloadable JSON file is available for download that can be opened in Revit.
